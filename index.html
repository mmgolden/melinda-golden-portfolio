About

I am passionate about JavaScript and building web applications. I have been working for a web design company for almost 3 years building static websites and answering support tickets. I am self-taught, and I decided to expand my skills by enrolling in an online program called the Treehouse Full Stack JavaScript Techdegree.

I started learning web development a few years ago. After graduating from art school, my husband and I started a wedding photography business. But we decided to pursue other passions and that’s when I became a marketing manager for a surgery office. I discovered that I enjoyed building the websites for the doctors. It was then that I began studying web development. And using my portfolio of websites, I was hired for my current role as a web developer.

At my current job, I build websites every day. And with my background in art, I have an eye for design. I’m highly self-motivated and I have a strong attention to detail. My goal is to build web applications with the best practices and latest technology.

Besides coding, I enjoy traveling and visiting new places, hiking in the mountains, photography, and spending time with my husband and dog.
